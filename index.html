Fun Fact about Queen Elizabeth II


  • She was the longest-reigning monarch in British history, serving for over 70 years.

  • She was the first British monarch to serve in the armed forces during World War II.

  • She was a trained mechanic and driver, having joined the Auxiliary Territorial Service during the war.

  • She was the first monarch to send an email, which she did in 1976.

  • She was a fan of corgis and owned more than 30 during her lifetime.

  • She was the first British monarch to visit China in 1986.

  • She was known for her love of horses and horse racing, and she owned several racehorses.

  • She was a great-grandmother and had eight grandchildren and twelve great-grandchildren.

  • She was a patron of over 600 charities and organizations.
